VITO (CH)

Dj & spreadhead of Swiss label Addictive Pleasure Real Healing, Vito is a passionate digger and music enthusiast. His love for the rythmn came from his adolescency where he used to play guitar. He then went into electronic music and fall in love with the scene. Part of the Swiss label APRH Recordings, he create his own style and party. Resident DJ at the famous Audio Club in Geneva and Folklor Club in Lausanne, he throw more than 50 parties inviting the big names of the industry.
